Recommended Etsy Size Strategy

This app now uses the most common printable wall art ratio packages: 2:3 for 4x6, 8x12, 12x18, 16x24, 24x36; 3:4 for 6x8, 9x12, 12x16, 18x24; 4:5 for 8x10, 11x14, 16x20; and ISO for A1, A2, A3, A4. The Etsy ZIP will place files into matching ratio folders.

Recommended Etsy Size Strategy

For printable wall art, your current size set is strong. The most useful bundles are 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, and common portrait sizes like 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, 16x20, 18x24, and 24x36. A-series sizes are helpful for international buyers, but they are optional. If you want to keep your shop simple, you can skip A sizes at first and add them later if customers ask.
